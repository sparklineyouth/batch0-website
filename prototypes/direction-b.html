How it works

Five steps to building a real company at batch0. The dates are set by the Cohort 1 calendar (Aug 17 – Oct 18).

[1/5]

Apply — free

Come with an idea you want to build, or just a desire to learn. We admit students who show they'll actually do the work, not just ideas.

[2/5]

Get a decision by email

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Each application is read and thoughtfully reviewed by our team.

[3/5]

Enroll — $130, only if accepted

That's it. Nothing more. Refund terms are published on the refund policy page.

[4/5]

Build season

Validate, build, market, pitch and most importantly, transform your idea into a company.

[5/5]

Pitch at demo day

The cohort closes with a live demo day where you present the company you built. Standouts may be offered batch0 sponsorship, a non-dilutive grant, decided on merit, never guaranteed.

What you leave with

By demo day your idea will look like this:

  • -rw-r--r-- lean-canvas.pdf validated in interviews with strangers who owe you nothing
  • -rwxr-xr-x shipped-v1.url landing page, no-code MVP, or working prototype — live
  • -rw-r--r-- business-model.xlsx revenue, pricing, and unit economics you can defend
  • -rw-r--r-- go-to-market.md a concrete path to your first paying customers
  • -rw-r--r-- pitch-deck.key written, rehearsed, and delivered live at demo day
  • drwx------ your-company/ 100% yours — no equity, no IP, no royalties taken

Who runs this

batch0 is built and run by Rishabh Dagli and Taran Bethi, two 17-year-old serial founders. We built batch0 to give high schoolers the same chance that we wish we had: to build a REAL company with mentorship and support, and even the chance of funding!

Cohort 1 is deliberately the first. There are no glossy alumni stats to show you yet, and we won't invent any. What we can promise: Rishabh and Taran run every live session themselves, read every application, and answer every parent question personally within a couple of days.

Questions parents ask

The short versions. Anything else: email a human.

├─ How much does it cost?
$0 to apply. $130 tuition, charged only if your student is accepted. No hidden fees, no equipment to buy, nothing else — ever.
├─ Who runs batch0?
Rishabh Dagli and Taran Bethi — the founders run every live session themselves. The legal entity is Sparkline Youth LLC.
├─ Who is it for?
U.S. high schoolers, ages 13–18, who want to build something real. No prior startup or coding experience required.
├─ How much time does it take?
Designed to fit around school: live sessions plus independent build time each week across the Aug 17 – Oct 18 season.
├─ Is it really fully online?
Yes — every session is live and online. No travel, nothing to install beyond a browser.
├─ What is demo day?
The final session: students pitch the company they built, live. Standouts may earn merit-based, non-dilutive sponsorship — never guaranteed.
└─ Does batch0 take equity or own my idea?
No. 0% equity, no IP claims, no royalties. Everything your student builds is theirs.
